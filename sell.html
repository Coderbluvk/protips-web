How to Buy Crypto

  1. Navigate to the Sell Crypto page from the menu after logging in.
  2. Select the cryptocurrency you want to sell from the dropdown menu (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum).
  3. Enter the amount of the selected cryptocurrency you wish to sell.
  4. Your wallet address will be automatically displayed. Ensure it is correct.
  5. View the QR code of your wallet for easy scanning (optional).
  6. Review the details and click the Sell Crypto button to confirm.
  7. Receive a confirmation message once the transaction is successful.
  8. The proceeds from the sale will be credited to your account or payment method.
