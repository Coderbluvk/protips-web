How to Buy Crypto

  1. Select the cryptocurrency you want to buy from the dropdown menu (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum).
  2. Enter the amount of the selected cryptocurrency you wish to purchase.
  3. Provide the wallet address where you want to receive the purchased cryptocurrency.
  4. (Optional) Upload a QR code of your wallet for faster processing.
  5. Review the details and click the Buy Crypto button to confirm.
  6. Complete the payment using your preferred payment method.
  7. Receive a confirmation message once the transaction is successful.
